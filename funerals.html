Funerals

Funerals and Memorials Services are
generally sad occasions and therefore
calls for music that is most fitting.

Planning Christian burials may take a
few days or longer but either way,
never wait for the last minute for
someone to provide the music.

If you are in need of an organist,
pianist or keyboard player,
contact me early, as part of your planning
and/or arrangements. Do this,
preferably, once the choice of songs
or hymns have been discussed and
not as a last minute thought.
Please use the contact details provided elsewhere.
