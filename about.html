About Me

Born in Fort Beaufort, 16 February 1957

At the age of 10 Gregory received his first pipe organ lessons from a Catholic nun,
Sister Victorina, at the Catholic Church of the town - St Michael

Inspired by his dad, the late Samuel Snayer who was regarded as one of the top conductors and choirmasters in the Eastern Cape,
Gregory composed a Mass that he calls The Mass of St Michael the Archangel, named after the local church,
a name he also adopted at his
Confirmation into the Catholic Church.
This Mass was penned in 1985 and first
sung in its entirety in 1987.

Now in his retirement years, Gregory
decided to continue composing Masses
for the Church. In fact, one has been
done in October, 2022, called The Mass
of The Nativity. Keep a watch out for more...
