Create

In the past, I found many people looking for sheet music that is hard to find or is simply not available. They may find the
relevant melody or song as an audio-
visual/video clip or an audio clip. Some
may remember the tune and is able to
sing it through. In the event of any of the
above, I am able to score/write (create)
the sheet music if you are desperate. I
can provide the melody line if that is
preferred, or a version with piano
accompaniment. EITHER of these CAN BE DONE!

Please bear in mind that depending on the complexity of the song, be prepared to wait a few days in (if necessary) for the completion of the sheet music that is needed.
