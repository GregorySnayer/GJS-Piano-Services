Weddings

Weddings, in most cases, take
months of preparations. Providing
music may play a small role but is
essential. The focus being on love,
the choice of songs should therefore
be appropriate. The person in
charge of the music for the day must
be informed of the details well in
advance and thoroughly discussed.

In the case of Catholics, there are a
few things a couple need to know
while planning the wedding. For
more information, contact me via
Whatsapp, SMS or email, or give
me a call.
